This product is a REF parser for CMFBibliographyAT

Dependencies: 

 * CMFBibliographyAT (Collective CVS)

Installation:

 * Copy/Move the product in your INSTANCE_HOME/Products folder, restart Zope.

 * From the ZMI, go to the 'Parsers' folder in the 'portal_bibliography' tool.

 * Choose 'Ref Parser' from the drop-down list and click 'Add'
